Student Analytics PoC

AI-driven student analytics and intervention engine

Welcome to the Student Analytics Platform

This proof-of-concept application enables educators to gain holistic insights into student performance and wellbeing by analyzing PASS (Pupil Attitudes to Self and School), CAT4 (Cognitive Abilities Test), and internal academic scores.

Upload your data files to generate comprehensive reports with recommended intervention strategies tailored to each student's needs.

Upload Data

Emotional & Behavioral Analysis

Identify students at risk in areas such as self-regard, attitude to teachers, work ethic, and emotional control based on PASS assessment data.

Cognitive Profile Assessment

Analyze cognitive strengths and weaknesses across verbal, quantitative, non-verbal, and spatial reasoning domains using CAT4 assessment data.

Academic Performance Insights

Compare academic achievement with cognitive potential to identify underperforming students and target appropriate interventions.

How It Works

1. Upload your PASS, CAT4, and academic data files.
2. Our AI engine analyzes and triangulates the data.
3. View interactive dashboards with grade-level insights.
4. Access detailed student profiles with tailored intervention recommendations.
5. Generate comprehensive PDF reports for each student.
